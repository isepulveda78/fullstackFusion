What is pottery?

Pottery refers to the art and craft of creating objects, usually functional, from clay that are shaped and fired at high temperatures to harden and preserve them.

By Israel Sepulveda - October 20, 2023

What are the different types of clay used in pottery?

Common types of clay used in pottery include earthenware, stoneware, and porcelain. Each has different properties and firing temperatures.

By Israel Sepulveda - October 20, 2023

How do I get started with pottery as a beginner?

Start by taking a beginner's pottery class, gathering essential tools and materials, and practicing basic hand-building or wheel-throwing techniques.

By Israel Sepulveda - October 20, 2023
